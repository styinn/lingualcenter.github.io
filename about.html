About Us


Lingual Center is institution dedicated to studying all languages in the world . This institution was founded on April 12 2023 and is located on Jl. Grand Nusa Indah Raya Blok i9 no.5. This institution is focused on providing easy, fun and fast language learning in terms of grammar, use of vocabulary and use of sentences properly, correctly and appropriately. This institution does not only focus on theory in linguistics but also how to use it appropriately, well and correctly in everyday life.
Our activity in learning is that students can understand concepts in a language theoretically and can use it with correct pronunciation. Where students are given motivation to be able to make sentences so that they can give opinions and provide information about what will be said. We not only provide regular education in literary theory but also provide an understanding of how to use vocabulary appropriately according to its intended purpose.
